Our new logo reflects our core beliefs.

We believe in a world that works for people, rather than the other way around. This simple, yet powerful belief has been at the heart of everything we do and every product we make.

Our new logo is not dramatically different, rather it’s an update of what millions of people look for and use every day, around the world. We want this update to confer a sense of momentum that embodies our vision of optimism, progress and empowerment. For our name, we chose a typeface, colors and shapes that are simpler, richer and more vibrant.

Connecting people to people is what we do. We’ve redesigned our "double P” monogram to reflect this connection. Our new monogram emphasizes the connection we create between People when they use our products to buy, sell, or send money to each other. And it’s been designed to effortlessly fit in both large or small spaces (like our iPhone app).

Our new logo will appear on all of our products, all over the world. We hope it is a reminder and a symbol of a new economy, a people-powered economy.
